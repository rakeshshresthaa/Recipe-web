Ultimate Cheeseburger Recipe

Create the perfect gourmet burger that will make your taste buds dance!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • 4 brioche buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Fresh lettuce
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

1

Prepare the Patties

Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape into patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper.

2

Cook the Patties

Heat a grill or pan over medium-high heat. Cook patties for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare.

3

Add Cheese

Place cheese slices on patties during the last 30 seconds of cooking to melt.

4

Toast the Buns

Lightly toast the buns on the grill or in a toaster until golden brown.

5

Assemble

Layer lettuce, tomato, onion, and the cheesy patty between the toasted buns.

Pro Tips

Use freshly ground beef for the best flavor and texture

Let the patties rest for 5 minutes before serving

Don't press down on the patties while cooking
